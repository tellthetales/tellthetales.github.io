Have you ever asked yourself how you are going to pass down all your treasured family photographs in a way that your children and grandchildren will know
 not only who is in the photo, but also what was significant about their lives?  Or perhaps you have a beloved parent, grandparent, aunt or uncle who has incredible stories to tell about their life, and you are wondering how to capture these tales before your opportunity is lost.


  
Each person and every family has a story to tell, lessons learned from struggles and triumphs, failures and successes.  Your story can be preserved for generations to come through video biography, a means of capturing not only the photographs and names, but also the person telling the story...their voice, facial expressions, special laugh and unique mannerisms.  This is indeed a priceless gift!

Create a video biography for a special event or as a Family Legacy Keepsake!

Tell the Tales helps you celebrate important events with DVDs which not only compile your family photos in a captivating way, but also allows you to tell the stories which make your photos come alive.  Each DVD can incorporate photos, home movies, music, documents, interviews and other material to create a family heirloom for generations to come.
